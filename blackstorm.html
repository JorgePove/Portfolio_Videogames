About the Game

An online multiplayer Battle Royale where the players form crews of three pirates to face each other in deadly combat. Sail with your space ship in a three-dimensional environment with total freedom of movement and rotation. Loot, equip your ship, shoot your cannons, board the enemy, sabotage their ship and blow them up. The last crew standing wins!
