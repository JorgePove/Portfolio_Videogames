FPS narrative driven single player AAA game

Project Name Squadron 42
Role Level Designer III
Date September 2020 – April 2025

  • Created level blockouts from whitebox stage all the way to final product, ensuring immersive and well-balanced gameplay experiences.

  • Worked alongside Art, Programming, Tech Design, VFX and Audio teams to ensure consistency and quality across all aspects of the level.
  • Experience with in-house game engine, StarEngine, using Blueprint-like scripting language.
  • Strong communication, teamwork and collaboration skills in multidisciplinary environments.
