Welcome

Level Designer  |  Game Designer

Scroll

I've always loved all kinds of videogames and the unique chance this craft has to make players live a thousand lives, feel a thousand emotions.

My aim is to make places capable of evoking those emotions. Whether it is awe, terror, a sense of wonder or an adrenaline rush, I believe it must be crafted from the very foundation through composition, pacing and curated interactions.

That's why I became a game designer, so I can create mechanics, rules and environments that can drive those experiences.

I'm a passionate and experienced level designer, dedicated for more than 6 years to crafting immersive gaming experiences that allow players to explore unique and vibrant worlds and feel profound emotions. I'm constantly pushing to hone my skills in level design, game design and cross-disciplinary collaboration and teamwork.

I'm committed to keep learning and improving my skills. My love for teamwork and passion for game development always drive me to try my hardest to make significant contributions to the success of every project I'm in, and make sure I create a work environment where people feel safe, valued and supported, so we can all do our best together. I enjoy working with multiple disciplines (art, code, animation, tech design, etc.) and I believe with my experience I can be a great fit for any team.

I like game design so much I even go beyond just videogames. I've created and designed multiple campaigns, puzzles and encounters for D&D games that I run with several groups and I'm currently working on my very own first board game!
